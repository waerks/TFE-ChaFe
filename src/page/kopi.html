Kopi, our coffee artisan

Welcome to the world of Kopi, a coffee artisan committed to an eco-responsible and ethical approach. At Kopi, they believe that the quality of your cup of coffee should not come at the expense of the environment or the coffee growing communities. That's why they are committed to working only with organic coffee producers and importing their coffee fairly.

Their organic coffee is grown without pesticides or chemical fertilisers, which helps to preserve biodiversity and soil health. By working directly with the producers, they are able to ensure that every cup of coffee you enjoy with us has a positive impact on the lives of those who grew it.

But their commitment doesn't stop there. At Kopi, they are also committed to minimising their environmental impact. That's why we use their compostable packaging and sustainable practices in all our operations.

We pride ourselves on being able to offer you a superior quality coffee that is good for you, good for the planet and good for the communities that produce it. Join us at ChaFé for an authentic and responsible coffee experience.
